So just WHO is dining at your feeder and WHAT is that splashing in your birdbath?

Well, DON'T pick up that old paper guidebook of birds. No, not any longer.

INSTEAD, pull out your phone, take a pic, then send it to the BirdSpecs AI visual recognition algorithm for INSTANT SPECIES IDENTIFICATION.

Hard-to-identify cases go to BirdSpecs members (the flock!) for identification, then are fed back to the algorithm to help it learn.

* Track which birds you've seen in your personal sightings log.

* Get alerted when you've spotted a species rare for your area.

* See other BirdSpecs member's photos and sightings logs. Share your photos and sightings. Chat with the flock.

* View maps of sightings.

* Help identify birds in difficult cases. Teach the AI!

FAQ

Is this for real? What the flock is going on here?
Sorry. This is not a crowdfunding effort for a real startup. This is a (possibly bird-brained) student project for Stanford's Startup Engineering course. But it would be cool if it were real. And not a half-bad idea at that, eh?
What are bitcoins?
bitcoins are the unit of currency of the Bitcoin system. Bitcoin is a system of owning and voluntarily transferring amounts of these so-called bitcoins, in a manner similar to an on-line banking, but without reliance on a central authority to maintain account balances.
Why bitcoins?
Bitcoins provide a universal currency for purpose of this peoject/class, as the class members are distributed worldwide.
